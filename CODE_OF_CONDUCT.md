# Code of Conduct

We follow the spirit of the Contributor Covenant:

- Be respectful and inclusive. No harassment, personal attacks, or discrimination.
- Assume good intent, disagree constructively, and keep discussions focused on the work.
- Protect user and operator safety; do not encourage insecure practices.
- Report conduct concerns to community@vision.dev.

Maintainers may moderate issues, PRs, and community spaces to enforce these guidelines.
