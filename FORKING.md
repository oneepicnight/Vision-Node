# Forking Vision

Vision is open source. Forking is allowed and expected.

However, clarity matters.

---

## What You MAY Do

- Fork the code
- Modify the implementation
- Experiment with features
- Run private or public networks

---

## What You MAY NOT Do

- Claim to be the official Vision network
- Reuse Vision branding or logos
- Use the Vision name to imply endorsement
- Masquerade as Vision infrastructure

---

## Network Identity

The official Vision network is defined by:
- Its genesis configuration
- Its chain identity
- Its maintainers

Forks must establish their own identity.

---

## Respect the Chain

Consensus rules exist to protect users.
Breaking them breaks trust.

Build boldly — but label honestly.
