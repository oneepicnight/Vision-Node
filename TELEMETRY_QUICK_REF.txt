===============================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
===============================================================================

Issue: Heatmap not showing
Fix:   Check VISION_GEOIP_DB path is correct
       Look for: "[GEOIP] Loaded GeoIP DB" in logs

Issue: Swarm viz not showing
Fix:   Set VISION_SWARM_VIZ=true in .env

Issue: Too many logs
Fix:   Remove VISION_SWARM_VIZ to disable
       Remove VISION_GEOIP_DB to disable heatmap
